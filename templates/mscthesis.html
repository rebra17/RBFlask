Space Grade Bidirectional Converter for Regeneration of Energy

During this project, the optimal topology for the application is determined and a solution for the energy storage system is determined. The proposed topology is a Dual Active Bridge converter, and the proposed energy solution consists of a supercapacitor bank with high current capabilities and energy density. The proposed energy solution can quickly be charged and discharged, which fits the application well.


A Dual Active Bridge converter rated for 1kW output power is designed with reliability in mind. The inductor is integrated into the transformer by designing the transformer with a large leakage inductance. An analogue peak-current mode controller is designed and implemented for inherent over-current protection. The controller is capable of operating in both directions of power-flow as well as controlling the direction of power-flow. A discrete logic circuitry is proposed for controlling the converter state as well as detecting faults and deactivating the system.
