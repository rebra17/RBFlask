Space Grade Bidirectional Converter for Regeneration of Energy

During this project, the optimal topology for the application was determined and a solution for the energy storage system was proposed. The proposed topology was a Dual Active Bridge converter, and the proposed energy store solution consisted of a supercapacitor bank with high current capabilities and energy density. The proposed energy store solution can quickly be charged and discharged, which fitted the application well at the time.


A Dual Active Bridge converter rated for 1kW output power was designed with reliability in mind. The inductor was integrated into the transformer by designing the transformer with a large leakage inductance. An analogue peak-current mode controller is designed and implemented for inherent over-current protection. The controller was capable of operating in both directions of power-flow as well as controlling the direction of power-flow. A discrete logic circuitry was proposed for controlling the converter state as well as detecting faults, such as over-current and over-voltage and deactivating the system.
