Motor and inverter test setup for a Formula Student Race Car

The purpose of this project was to create a platform/test-bench setup for motor control using the dSPACE device MicroLabBox.
The project covered design and decisions made selecting hardware, keeping safery regulations in terms of the Danish Standards in mind. The project was carried out in collaboration with SDU-Vikings Racing team and University of Southern Denmark, with setup simulated and tested.


A focus of the project was to ease the inrtroduction of dSPACE and control of the motor in order to optimise and implement control methods presented in the project.
