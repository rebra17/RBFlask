TS to LV DCDC

During this project, a HV (588V) to LV (24V) isolated DCDC converter was designed. The project carried out over 4 months (1 semester).


The primary and secondary-side topologies was selected as Full-bridge (FB)-FB with SiliconCarbide (SiC) and Gallium-Nitride (GaN) - technology respectively, based on the power losses and transformer utilisation.


Planar transformer and inductor was designed, in order to minimise the height of the final PCB and to be able to control parasitic parameters as tightly as possible. Filters, snubber-circuit, ICL and other protection was implemented to ensure the reliability of the converter and the peak-efficiency was derived to 96.03% at nominal voltage at a switching frequency of 200kHz.
